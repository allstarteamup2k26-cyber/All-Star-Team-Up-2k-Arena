How It Works

Simple. Fair. Competitive.

01

Pick Your Tournament

Choose your game (2K25 or 2K26) and your console (PS4 or PS5). Each tournament has its own bracket and $450 prize pool ($300 / $100 / $50).

02

Register & Pay

Teams pay $50 for all 5 players. Solo players pay $15 and get placed on a balanced team by skill level. Payment via Stripe — secure and instant.

03

Round Robin First

Every team plays 3 group stage games. Your record earns your seed. Best W-L record = top seed. Tiebreaker is point differential.

04

Double Elimination Bracket

All 16 teams enter the bracket seeded by their group stage record. Lose once → Losers Bracket. Lose twice → eliminated. Grand Finals will be broadcast live on Twitch & YouTube. All other results & standings posted live in Discord.
