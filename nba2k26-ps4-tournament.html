About the
Tournament

The All-Star Team-Up 2K Arena | Private 5v5 Player Control Tournaments is the first event of its kind — bringing together competitive 2K players from all skill levels in a structured, professional bracket tournament with real prize money on the line.

Whether you've got a squad ready or you're rolling solo, there's a spot for you. Full teams pay $50 to enter as a unit. Solo players pay $15 and get placed on a balanced team with other solo registrants before the bracket drops.

Every team plays 3 Round Robin games first — records and point differential determine your seed. Then all 16 teams drop into a full Double Elimination bracket. One loss doesn't end your run. Every result is posted live. Grand Finals will be broadcast live. All other match results and standings will be posted live in Discord. This is organized, fair, and built to grow every season.

Section 03

Official Rules

01

🏟 Locker Room Setup

The higher-seeded team captain creates the locker room and invites the opposing captain. Before tip-off, the locker room owner must screenshot the game settings and submit it with the match result. All settings below are mandatory — no exceptions:

Required Settings:
🏀 Mode: All-Star Team Up
🔒 Privacy: Private
⚠️ Difficulty: Hall of Fame
⏱ Quarter Length: 5 Minutes
🗺 Teams: West vs East

Any match started with incorrect settings is immediately void. The team responsible for the wrong setup forfeits that game with no replay.

03

Registration & Payment

Teams pay $50 (captain pays once for all 5). Solo players pay $15 each. Spots confirmed after payment only. No refunds after bracket seeding. One registration per player — no roster sharing.

04

Roster Rules

Each team must have exactly 5 registered players. You may designate 1 substitute at registration — no mid-tournament additions. The substitute must be registered and paid before the bracket is released.

05

Age Requirement

All players must be 18+. Players aged 13–17 may compete with written parental consent submitted at registration. Players under 13 are not eligible. Required for prize payout compliance.

06

Match Format

Phase 1 — Round Robin: Every team plays exactly 3 group stage games. All 16 teams participate. Records (W-L) determine seeding. Tiebreaker: point differential. Phase 2 — Double Elimination: All 16 teams seeded into a double elimination bracket. Best of 3 per match, Best of 5 in the Grand Finals. Lose once → Losers Bracket. Lose twice → eliminated. Grand Finals reset applies if the Losers Bracket champion wins.

07

Scheduling

Captains have 48 hours to coordinate match times after bracket release. All scheduling confirmed in the #scheduling Discord channel. Failure to schedule within 48 hours = team forfeit. All times in ET.

19

Substitutes & Stand-ins

Only your registered substitute may replace a player. Unregistered stand-ins are not permitted under any circumstances. If a team cannot field 5 registered players at match time, they forfeit.

19

Reporting Results

The winning captain must post a screenshot or score in #results within 1 hour of match completion. Both captains must confirm. Unconfirmed results held 24 hours before organizer review.

19

⚠ No Cheating

Any use of exploits, hacks, macros, or outside assistance results in immediate team disqualification and permanent bans for all involved. Reports require video evidence. No warnings — first offense is final.

19

⚠ Code of Conduct

Harassment, hate speech, slurs, or targeted toxicity in any channel results in removal of the offending player and potential team disqualification. Captains are responsible for their roster's behavior.

19

Disconnections

Disconnect within first 60 seconds = round may restart once per match. After 60 seconds or after 1 restart, the disconnect stands. Teams continue with available players.

19

Equipment

Each player is responsible for their own hardware, internet, and power. Technical failures are not grounds for match replay beyond the one-restart rule. Prepare your setup before every match.

19

Stream Sniping

All streamed matches require a minimum 2-minute broadcast delay. Watching your own live stream during a match for tactical information is cheating, handled under Rule 08.

19

Disputes

Submit disputes in #disputes within 2 hours of match completion with evidence. Organizer decisions are final. Toxic behavior during disputes = automatic forfeit of the dispute regardless of merit.

19

Prize Payout

Paid to winning team captain via PayPal/Venmo within 7 days of Finals. Captain distributes to teammates. 30-day claim window — unclaimed prizes are forfeited. Identity verification required.

19

Solo Queue Players

Solo players are assigned to teams before the bracket is seeded. Once assigned, all standard team rules apply. No team switches after assignment. No refunds after team assignment is announced.

19

Rule Acknowledgment

All registered players are bound by these rules at registration regardless of whether they've read them. Rules may be updated before the tournament starts — players notified in #announcements.

🎲 How Seeding & Format Works

Two-phase format. Seeding is earned on the court — not assigned randomly. Here's exactly how it works:

Phase 1 Every team plays 3 Round Robin games during the Group Stage (July 31, 2026). Records are tracked publicly in real time.
Seeding After 3 games, all 16 teams are ranked by W-L record. Tiebreaker is point differential (total points scored minus total points allowed across all 3 games).
Phase 2 All 16 teams enter the Double Elimination bracket seeded 1–32. Seed 1 vs Seed 32, Seed 2 vs Seed 31, and so on.
Winners Win in the Winners Bracket → stay in Winners Bracket. Lose once → drop to Losers Bracket. Lose twice → eliminated.
Grand Finals Winners Bracket champion vs Losers Bracket champion. If the Losers side wins, a bracket reset game is played — both teams have 1 loss so it's a true final.
Bracket Full seeded bracket published TBD after group stage ends. Captains have 24 hours to raise seeding disputes with evidence.
